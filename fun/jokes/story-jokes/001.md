A priest, a rabbi, and a bartender walk into a bar.

The bartender looks up and says, "What is this, some kind of joke?"

The priest says, "Well, actually, we're here for the trivia night."

The rabbi nods, "I heard first place wins a free appetizer platter."

The bartender sighs, pours himself a drink, and says, "Guys, I work here. I should've called in sick."
