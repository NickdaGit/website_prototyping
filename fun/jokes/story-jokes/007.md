Two hikers are lost in the woods. One of them pulls out a gun and fires three shots into the air.

"It's a universal distress signal," he explains. "Someone will come help us."

They wait an hour. Nothing happens. He fires three more shots into the air.

Another hour passes. Still nothing.

"Try again," says the other hiker.

"Okay," says the first hiker, "but I'm almost out of arrows."
