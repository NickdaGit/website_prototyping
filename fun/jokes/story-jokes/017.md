A painting contractor is interviewing people for a job. He asks the first applicant, "Do you have any experience?"

"I once painted a whole church in two days," says the man.

"Impressive! You're hired."

After the first day, the contractor checks: only one wall is finished. After the second day, it's barely two walls.

He asks, "You said you painted a whole church in two days!"

"I did," the man replies, "but the church was in a painting, and the painting was very small."
