An employer is interviewing three candidates. He asks each the same question: "What is two plus two?"

The first candidate says, "Four." The employer nods.

The second candidate says, "It could be 22." The employer raises an eyebrow.

The third candidate closes the door, draws the blinds, leans in, and whispers, "What do you want it to be?"

He got the job.
