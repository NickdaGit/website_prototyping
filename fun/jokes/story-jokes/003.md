A man finds a magic lamp on the beach and rubs it. A genie pops out.

"I'll grant you three wishes," says the genie, "but whatever you wish for, your ex-wife gets double."

The man thinks carefully. "First, I wish for a billion dollars."

"Done," says the genie. "Your ex-wife now has two billion."

"Second, I wish for a mansion on every continent."

"Done. Your ex-wife has two mansions on every continent."

The man smiles. "For my third wish... I wish you'd scare me half to death."
