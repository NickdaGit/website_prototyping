A mechanical engineer, an electrical engineer, and a software engineer are driving when their car breaks down.

The mechanical engineer says, "It's probably the engine. Let me check under the hood."

The electrical engineer says, "No, it's likely a wiring issue. Let me check the fuses."

The software engineer says, "Why don't we all get out of the car and get back in again?"
