A new CEO finds three numbered envelopes in his desk drawer from the outgoing CEO with instructions: "Open these in order when you get into trouble."

After six months, sales tank. He opens Envelope 1: "Blame your predecessor." He does, and it buys him time.

A year later, profits crash. He opens Envelope 2: "Reorganize the company." He does, and things stabilize.

Two years in, disaster strikes again. He opens Envelope 3: "Prepare three envelopes."
