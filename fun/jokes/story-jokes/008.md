A guy hears a knock at his door. He opens it and sees a snail on his doorstep.

Annoyed, he picks up the snail and throws it as far as he can across the yard.

Three years later, there's another knock at his door. He opens it, and there's the snail again.

The snail looks up at him and says, "What the heck was that about?!"
