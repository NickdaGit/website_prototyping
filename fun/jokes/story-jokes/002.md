A guy walks into a bar with his dog and says, "I'll bet you a round of drinks that my dog can talk."

The bartender says, "Sure, go ahead."

The guy asks the dog, "What's on top of a house?" The dog says, "Roof!"

The bartender rolls his eyes. The guy asks, "How does sandpaper feel?" The dog says, "Rough!"

The bartender is getting annoyed. "One more chance," he says.

The guy asks, "Who was the greatest baseball player?" The dog says, "Ruth!"

The bartender kicks them both out. Sitting on the sidewalk, the dog looks at his owner and says, "Should I have said DiMaggio?"
