A physicist, an engineer, and an economist are stranded on a desert island with nothing but canned food.

The physicist says, "Let's heat the cans on the rocks until the pressure pops the lids."

The engineer says, "Let's build a lever system to puncture the cans."

The economist says, "First, let's assume we have a can opener."
