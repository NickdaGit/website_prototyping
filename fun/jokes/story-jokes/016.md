A chicken walks into a library, goes up to the desk, and says, "Buk."

The librarian hands it a book. It walks out.

A minute later the chicken comes back: "Buk buk." The librarian hands it two books.

This goes on all afternoon. Finally, the curious librarian follows the chicken to a pond where a frog is sitting on a lily pad.

The chicken drops the books in front of the frog. The frog looks at each one and says, "Reddit... reddit... reddit..."
