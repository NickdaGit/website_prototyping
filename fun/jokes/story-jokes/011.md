A man walks into a bar with a dog. The bartender says, "No dogs allowed."

The man says, "But this dog can talk!"

The bartender says, "Prove it and drinks are on the house."

The man turns to the dog: "What's on top of a house?" The dog says, "Roof!"

"What does sandpaper feel like?" — "Rough!"

"Who was the greatest baseball player?" — "Ruth!"

The bartender throws them both out.

The dog looks up at the man and says, "Should I have said DiMaggio?"
