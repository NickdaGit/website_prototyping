A photographer arrives at a dinner party carrying his equipment. The host opens the door and says, "Oh, what lovely photos you take! You must have a really expensive camera."

The photographer says nothing.

At the end of the evening, as he's leaving, the photographer turns to the host and says, "That was a wonderful dinner. You must have some really expensive pots."
