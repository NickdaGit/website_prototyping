A man finds a wallet with $700 in it. The next day, he sees a notice at the church: "Lost wallet with $700. Sentimental value. Please return."

He goes to the address on the notice and returns the wallet. The owner counts the money and says, "I see you've already taken your reward — there was $800 in here."

"That's impossible!" says the man.

"Alright," says the owner, "let's let the pastor decide."

The pastor thinks and says, "The wallet you describe had $800. This wallet has $700. Therefore this cannot possibly be your wallet. Sir, if someone returns a wallet with $700, you must wait for the one with $800."
