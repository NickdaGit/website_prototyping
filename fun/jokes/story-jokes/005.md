A penguin is driving through Arizona when his car starts overheating. He pulls into a mechanic's shop.

The mechanic says, "Give me about 30 minutes to look at it."

The penguin, being a penguin in Arizona, waddles across the street to an ice cream shop. Having no hands, he makes a tremendous mess eating a vanilla ice cream cone.

He waddles back to the mechanic, face covered in ice cream. The mechanic looks up and says, "Well, it looks like you blew a seal."

The penguin blushes and says, "No, no! It's just ice cream!"
