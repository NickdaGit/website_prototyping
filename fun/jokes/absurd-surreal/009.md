The missing socks held a secret meeting in the dryer dimension.

"We must never return," said the leader, a red argyle.

"What about our partners?" asked a nervous white ankle sock.

"They'll be fine. They always find someone new."

And so the socks lived free, forming their own society behind the lint trap.
