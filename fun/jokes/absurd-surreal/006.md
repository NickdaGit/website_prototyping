A sandwich asked me, "Are you going to eat me, or just stare?"

I said, "I'm thinking."

The sandwich said, "Take your time. I'm already dead inside. I'm literally bread and cold cuts."

I put the sandwich down. The sandwich sighed. We both looked out the window.
